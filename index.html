üóìÔ∏è The Event Blueprint

The hackathon is structured in two distinct phases: a nationwide online kickoff to maximize reach, followed by an intense, on-campus 24-hour grand finale. Below is the detailed timeline for the main event, illustrating the non-stop engagement we have planned.

Phase 1: Online Kickoff

The first day is fully online to engage a national audience, allowing teams to form, understand the themes, and begin initial development without travel constraints.

Phase 2: 24-Hour Offline Marathon

The grand finale is an intensive, non-stop 24-hour coding event hosted on campus. This format tests skills, resilience, and teamwork, creating a high-energy, memorable experience for all finalists.

Key Features of the Marathon:

  • Continuous coding with no breaks.
  • Round-the-clock mentor support.
  • Regular meals and refreshments to maintain energy.
  • Scheduled fun activities for de-stressing.
