üéØ Core Objective

This section outlines the primary goal of the hackathon: to create a dynamic environment for technological innovation and skill development among students in the Kanpur region.

To provide a structured and competitive platform for students from the Kanpur region to showcase their technical and problem-solving skills. The event will challenge participants to build real-world, innovative solutions using technology, thereby fostering a culture of creativity, collaboration, and hands-on learning that bridges academic knowledge with practical application.

üóìÔ∏è Event Schedule & Blueprint

The hackathon is structured over four days, blending online development with an offline finale. Explore the day-by-day plan and the technology themes participants can work on.

Kickoff & Foundation

  • Official Hackathon kickoff and welcome address.
  • Announcement of themes and problem statements.
  • Team registration and formation portal opens.
  • Mandatory GitHub repository creation for all teams.

Development & Progress

  • Full day dedicated to project development.
  • Mentor support sessions available via Discord/Google Meet.
  • Daily progress submission required via GitHub commits.
  • Workshops on relevant technologies (optional).

Finalization & Submission

  • Final hours of project development and refinement.
  • Project submission deadline.
  • Submissions must include a detailed README file.
  • Screenshots and a short demo video are mandatory.

Presentation & Finale

  • In-person project presentations at the department.
  • Evaluation by a distinguished panel of judges.
  • Networking session with judges and faculty.
  • Awards ceremony and announcement of results.
